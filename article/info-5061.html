位置 Position：上海

起点 Start：中原路开鲁路

终点 End：上海交大闵行校区/世纪公园

任务性质 Property of missions：Multiple (3863 Hacks,55 Capture/Upgrade,2 Links,1 MOD,35 Waypoints,48 Questions)

典型完成时间 Typical duration required：20 Days（558.1KM）


这是目前全世界最长的Ingress组图任务之一，共648个任务。它的艰巨性可以由总路程（558公里）和预计用时（20天）看出来，事实上即使花20天去做任务，每天也要走大约30公里也即4万步。我们并不建议你去尝试，但你可能有兴趣知道它并作为某种谈资。


《清明上河图》是北宋张择端的作品，长5287mm，高252mm。它描绘的是北宋时期（920~1127）东京（即今天的开封）的居民生态，因其独特的创作手法而闻名。在创作时它被进贡给当时的皇帝宋徽宗，此后又历经转手并曾多次被临摹、仿造，如今有数个存世版本。本任务所对应的版本是最初版，原件收藏于北京故宫博物院。


It's one of the biggest quest series of the world.It contains 648 quests,which lasted for about 560 kilometres(or 350 miles).If you spent 8 hours each day with a 30 kilometres walk(about 40,000 steps),you need 20 days to complete.So we don't recommend you for it,but although you're in Shanghai,you should know it as knowledge.


The picture of quest is a famous one,'Qing Ming Shang He Tu'('Picture of Bian Jing).It has a 5287 mm length and 252 height,described many events around the river of East Capitol 'Bian Jing',the Song Dynasty(A.D. 920~1127).When it finished it was given to Zhao Ji,the Emperor,and the emperpr named it himself.Now this picture is storing in Gu Gong Meseum.
