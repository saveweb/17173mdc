“” 南京欢迎你组图任务

[Ingress17173原创专稿，未经允许请勿转载！]


设计者 Owner：CaptainArt

任务数量 No. of missions：18

Portal数量 No. of portals：108 （90 Unique）

起点 Start：地铁三号线从夫子庙站2号口

终点 End：玄武门站

任务性质 Property of missions：Multiple (34 Hacks,54 Capture/Upgrade,2 Mod,18 Questions)

典型完成时闲 Typical duration required：3~5 Hours (12.5KM)


注意事项 Notice：

1、一共18个任务，请从任务编号第18开始倒序完成。

2、不要做任何与任务无关的操作，尤其是不要随手插满高级脚、mod；不要随手入侵。

3、由于路线已经设计好与南京地铁线路密切关联，建议非南京本地玩家采用地铁作为主要交通方式。

4、iOS系统看不到完整的问题，请尝试A-F选项

任务路线：

任务分为三个阶段，每个阶段6个任务。


阶段一：历史文脉篇•夫子庙

坐地铁三号线从夫子庙站2号口出来后即可搜到任务18，开始拼图任务的旅程。任务18~13全部在夫子庙景区穿行并经过水平方、水游城，任务13（可骑车，夫子庙北门取自行车至三山街地铁站还车，坐地铁）的最后回引导玩家来到地铁一号线三山街站。


阶段二：现代都市篇•鼓楼

坐地铁一号线到鼓楼站，从1号口出来后即可搜到任务12。任务12~7依次经过鼓楼医院、鼓楼公园、紫峰大厦、鼓楼广场等地，最后回到鼓楼地铁站。


阶段三：景观风光篇•玄武湖

继续坐地铁一号线到玄武门站，从1号口出来即可搜到任务6。任务6~1会引导玩家从玄武湖公园进入后，绕樱洲（玄武湖中心四洲中最大的一个）一圈，最后回到玄武门站。


以上所有任务路线均为唯一。


It's a 18 quests series by descender order.The owner give some advice here:


*The quest series contain three parts,which one is 6 quests.In a part you can walk or take bicycle,between two parts,you'd better take metro.The beginning(the 18th)  is at Line 3 metro,Fu Zi Miao station,Exit 2.


*The part1 is the 'Culture'.Some old-styled building here,you can read the history of Nanjing,which is the Capitol of many dynasties of China.For example,the beginning subway station 'Fu Zi Miao' means 'The temple of Confucius' -- one of 4 biggest Confucius Temples of China -- for memories and praying.Confucius is a great thinker and educator,in about 2000 years,all Chinese students must learn the spirit of Confucius to improve themselves and that's the only way to get money and power.


*The part2 is the 'Modern Nanjing',showing you something of skyscrapes,parks or squares.


*The part3 is the landviews.You see the 'Xuan Wu' lake and surround it. 'Xuan Wu' is a mystic creature in Chinese tales,it's a snake binding with a turtle.Both the two are dark.It has a meaning 'Steady' or 'Guardian'.In Chinese tales it's one of the 4 holy creatures,landed at the North.
