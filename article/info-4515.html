“” 丰台花园系列任务


所有者 Owner: LZKLC

任务数 Nr. of missions：24

portal数量 Nr. of portals：97

位置 Position：丰台花园 总部基地

起点 Start：丰台花园

终点 End：总部基地

任务性质 Property of missions：Multiple (118 Hacks,2 Capture/Upgrade,2 Links,2 Questions)

典型完成时间 Typical duration required：4~5 Hours (14.6KM)

建议交通方式 Suggested means of transportation：步行+骑车


注意事项 Notice：

起点在丰台花园门口，门票1元。进去往右边走，转一圈出来沿着四环往南走，第一个路口后过马路坐2站公交到看丹桥（车很多，基本随便一辆都行）下车，继续沿着四环往南走，走到总部基地溜达一圈往南继续走到世界公园，门口免费po摸一遍回头朝总部基地方向走，走到麦当劳附近结束整个系列任务。（可以进去喝点东西歇息下）

PS.部分po既是任务结束po又是下一任务开始po，一个hack一个攻占或升级，建议先做任务操作再插脚，避免插满脚无法升级。


This quest series locate in the Feng Tai District,South Beijing.The picture is a Japanese anime character,'The Black Rock Shooter'.


This quest series probably request  some Beijing native taffic knowledge,please notice that.For example,when you leave the Feng Tai garden,you should take a bus (anyone) to Kan Dan Bridge,2 stops.If you can't read Chinese that may be some trouble.Or you have to go by your pure legs for 1,000 metres about,and there are very little potrals on the way.


The ticket of Feng Tai garden is 1 CNY.
